Government Agriculture Schemes


Our mission is to support farmers and promote sustainable agriculture in our country. We offer a range of services to help farmers improve their productivity and profitability, while also protecting the environment and preserving our natural resources.


Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board (MSAMB) - This scheme aims to establish a market-led, high value agriculture and allied products sector in the state. It provides support to farmers by creating modern infrastructure, enhancing value chain management, and facilitating marketing of agricultural products. For more information, visit: Maharashtra State Agricultural Marketing Board (MSAMB)

Chief Minister's Agriculture Solar Feeder Scheme - This scheme aims to provide uninterrupted power supply to farmers for their agricultural needs through solar energy. Under this scheme, the state government provides solar panels and batteries to farmers at subsidized rates. For more information, visit: cm-solar-agriculture-feeder-scheme

National Agricultural Insurance Scheme (NAIS) - This scheme provides financial assistance to farmers in case of crop losses due to natural calamities or pests/diseases. The scheme is implemented by the Maharashtra State Agricultural Department. For more information, visit: national-agricultural-insurance-scheme

Maharashtra State Seed Certification Agency - This scheme ensures the availability of quality seeds to farmers by certifying the seed production and distribution process. The agency also provides technical assistance and training to seed growers and processors. For more information, visit: maharashtra-state-seed-certification-agency

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) : It is a financial inclusion scheme launched on August 28, 2014, aimed at providing financial services to the unbanked population of India. You can find more information on this scheme on the official website of PMJDY. For more information, visit: Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) : It is a health insurance scheme launched on September 23, 2018, that aims to provide free health coverage to economically weaker sections of society. You can find more information on this scheme on the official website of AB-PMJAY: Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan : It is a cleanliness campaign launched on October 2, 2014, aimed at achieving a clean and open defecation-free India. You can find more information on this campaign on the official website of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan: Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Digital India : It is a program launched on July 1, 2015, aimed at transforming India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. You can find more information on this program on the official website of Digital India: Digital India
